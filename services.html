What we offer?

These features collectively empower organizations to streamline their financial processes, reduce cash handling, improve transparency, and gain valuable insights into their financial performance through mPurse.

Dashboard Analytics

Access a centralized dashboard that provides real-time analytics and insights into your organization's financial health. Monitor Mpesa statements, budget statements, transaction history, and more from a single, user-friendly interface

Branch and Department Management

Seamlessly manage multiple branches within your organization. Each branch can have its own departments and budgets, allowing you to maintain control and visibility at every level

Staff Management with Role-Based Permissions

Effortlessly manage your staff members, assign roles, and control access to different modules based on their responsibilities. Ensure that the right people have the right level of access

In-App Messaging

Foster efficient communication among your organization's staff members with integrated messaging. Discuss transactions, approvals, and other important matters within the system for increased collaboration

Advance Management

Enable staff members to request advances, which can be paid securely via Mpesa. Administer and approve these requests, and allow staff to add expenses to their advances, simplifying financial processes

Expense Tracking

Streamline expense tracking and management. Staff members can submit direct expenses for approval, which are then audited for accuracy. This ensures that expenses are properly documented and compliant

Financial Reporting

Generate comprehensive financial reports that include Mpesa balances, opening balances, total replenishments, open and closed advances, direct expenses, refunds, claims, and Mpesa transactional charges. Gain valuable insights into your organization's financial performance

Replenishment Management

Easily replenish your Mpesa account balance directly through mPurse. Keep your Mpesa funds at the optimal level for hassle-free transactions

Cash in Hand Report

Generate a downloadable report that provides a complete overview of your organization's financial status. This report includes essential details such as Mpesa balance, opening balance, transaction history, and more

Running Balance

Access a chronological list of all transactions within your organization. Maintain a transparent record of financial activities for audit and tracking purposes

Settings and Customization

Administrators can easily customize the platform to suit their organization's needs. Manage organization details, upload logos, configure Mpesa B2C credentials, set advance and expenditure limits, and enhance account security with password management.

Session Management

Manage organization sessions based on user count and duration (monthly, quarterly, or annually). Keep your subscription plans and billing cycles organized

How to getting started with mPurse?

These three steps guide you through the process of getting started with mPurse. From creating your account to taking control of your organization's financial management, mPurse simplifies the journey, putting you in command of your financial future.

Create your mPurse Account

Start by providing your organization's details through a simple form submission. Your information sets the foundation for your mPurse account

Verification and administrative credentials

After submitting your details, our team ensures security by verifying your organization. Once verified, you'll receive an email with administrative credentials, granting you access to mPurse as the administrator.

Take control of your Financial Management

As the administrator, customize mPurse by creating branches, departments, budgets, and adding staff. With your organization's structure in place, begin using mPurse to manage advances, expenses, and transactions efficiently, embracing cashless convenience and transparent financial control.
