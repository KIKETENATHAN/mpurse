How does Mpesa integration work with mPurse?

mPurse seamlessly integrates with Mpesa, allowing organizations to make cashless payments, receive refunds, and maintain transaction records directly within the platform. This integration simplifies financial transactions and enhances security.

Can I manage multiple branches and departments within mPurse?

Yes, mPurse supports multi-branch organizations. You can create branches, departments, and budgets, and assign staff members with role-based permissions for each branch. This flexibility ensures that mPurse adapts to your organizational structure.

Is my data secure with mPurse?

Absolutely. Data security is a top priority for us. mPurse employs robust security measures to protect your data. We use encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to maintain the highest level of data security.
