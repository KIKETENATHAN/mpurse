About us

Our story : mPurse

mPurse emerged as a startup with a mission: to modernize how Kenyan organizations handle finances. Inspired by Mpesa, they crafted a platform for seamless cash management, budget tracking, and expense control. With dedication and local partnerships, mPurse launched to a resounding reception in Kenya, simplifying financial processes and encouraging cashless transactions. Today, mPurse empowers Kenyan businesses to navigate a changing financial landscape with ease and confidence.
